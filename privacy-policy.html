Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Astrocore Terminal Fun Pack is a collection of offline terminal games and commands. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users.

  • No personal data collection
  • No cookies or tracking
  • No user accounts required
  • All games run locally on your device

2. How We Use Information

Since we don't collect any personal information, we don't use, share, or sell any user data. All terminal commands and games are designed to run entirely on your local machine.

3. Third-Party Services

Some commands may connect to external services:

  • ascii.live - For ASCII animations (when using curl commands)
  • towel.blinkenlights.nl - For Star Wars ASCII movie

These services have their own privacy policies and terms of use.

4. Data Security

All games and commands run locally on your device. No data is transmitted to our servers. Your privacy and security are maintained by the offline nature of our terminal games.
